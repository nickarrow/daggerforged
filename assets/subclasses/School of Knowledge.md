# School of Knowledge

Play the School of Knowledge if you want a keen understanding of the world around you.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Knowledge

### FOUNDATION FEATURES

**_Prepared:_** Take an additional domain card of your level or lower from a domain you have access to.

**_Adept:_** When you Utilize an Experience, you can **mark a Stress** instead of spending a Hope. If you do, double your Experience modifier for that roll.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SUBCLASS ABILITIES

**[Foundation]** _Prepared:_ At character creation, unlock one additional domain ability at your tier from a domain you have access to. This ability counts toward your 5-ability loadout limit. _Adept:_ When you leverage a relevant background or experience on a roll, you may **suffer -1 Spirit** instead of spending momentum. If you do, add +2 to the roll instead of +1.

**[Specialization]** _Accomplished:_ Unlock one additional domain ability at your tier from a domain you have access to. This ability counts toward your 5-ability loadout limit. _Perfect Recall:_ Once per scene, when you swap a vaulted domain ability into your active loadout, reduce its Recall Cost by 1 momentum (minimum 0).

**[Mastery]** _Brilliant:_ Unlock one additional domain ability at your tier from a domain you have access to. This ability counts toward your 5-ability loadout limit. _Honed Expertise:_ When you leverage a relevant background or experience on a roll, roll your action die first. On 5+, you gain the bonus without spending momentum.

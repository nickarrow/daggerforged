# Winged Sentinel

Play the Winged Sentinel if you want to take flight and strike crushing blows from the sky.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Strength

### FOUNDATION FEATURE

**_Wings of Light:_** You can fly. While flying, you can do the following:

- **Mark a Stress** to pick up and carry another willing creature approximately your size or smaller.
- **Spend a Hope** to deal an extra **1d8** damage on a successful attack.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED FOUNDATION FEATURE

**_Wings of Light:_** You can fly. While flying, you can do the following:

- **Suffer -1 Spirit** to pick up and carry another willing creature approximately your size or smaller.
- **Spend 1 Hope** when you Strike or Clash and score a hit to mark progress an additional time.

### SPECIALIZATION FEATURE

**_Ethereal Visage:_** Your supernatural visage strikes awe and fear. While flying, you have advantage on Presence Rolls. When you succeed with Hope on a Presence Roll, you can remove a Fear from the GM's Fear pool instead of gaining Hope.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SPECIALIZATION FEATURE

**_Ethereal Visage:_** Your supernatural visage strikes awe and fear. While flying, add +2 when you roll +Heart. When you Compel or make a social roll while flying and your action die shows 4+, take +1 momentum.

### MASTERY FEATURES

**_Ascendant:_** Gain a permanent +4 bonus to your Severe damage threshold.

**_Power of the Gods:_** While flying, you deal an extra **1d12** damage instead of 1d8 from your "Wings of Light" feature.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED MASTERY FEATURES

**_Ascendant:_** Reduce all harm you suffer by 1 (minimum 1).

**_Power of the Gods:_** While flying, when you use Wings of Light to spend Hope for extra progress, mark progress twice instead of once (for a total of three times on a hit).

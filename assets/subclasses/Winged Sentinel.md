# Winged Sentinel

Play the Winged Sentinel if you want to take flight and strike crushing blows from the sky.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Strength

### FOUNDATION FEATURE

**_Wings of Light:_** You can fly. While flying, you can do the following:

- **Mark a Stress** to pick up and carry another willing creature approximately your size or smaller.
- **Spend a Hope** to deal an extra **1d8** damage on a successful attack.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SUBCLASS ABILITIES

**[Foundation]** _Wings of Light:_ You can fly. While flying, you can do the following: **Suffer -1 Spirit** to pick up and carry another willing creature approximately your size or smaller. **Spend 1 Hope** when you Strike or Clash and score a hit to mark progress an additional time.

**[Specialization]** _Ethereal Visage:_ Your supernatural visage strikes awe and fear. While flying, add +2 when you roll +Heart. When you Compel or make a social roll while flying and your action die shows 4+, take +1 momentum.

**[Mastery]** _Ascendant:_ Reduce all harm you suffer by 1 (minimum 1). _Power of the Gods:_ While flying, when you use Wings of Light to spend Hope for extra progress, mark progress twice instead of once (for a total of three times on a hit).

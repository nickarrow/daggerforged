# Divine Wielder

Play the Divine Wielder if you want to dominate the battlefield with a legendary weapon.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Strength

### FOUNDATION FEATURES

**_Spirit Weapon:_** When you have an equipped weapon with a range of Melee or Very Close, it can fly from your hand to attack an adversary within Close range and then return to you. You can **mark a Stress** to target an additional adversary within range with the same attack roll.

**_Sparing Touch:_** Once per long rest, touch a creature and clear 2 Hit Points or 2 Stress from them.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SUBCLASS ABILITIES

**[Foundation]** _Spirit Weapon:_ Your melee weapon can fly from your hand to strike a foe you can see and return to you. When you Strike using your spirit weapon at range, roll +Iron. You may **suffer -1 Spirit** to target an additional foe with the same roll; if you hit, mark progress against both. _Sparing Touch:_ Once per session, touch a creature and restore +2 health or +2 Spirit to them (your choice).

**[Specialization]** _Devout:_ When you spend a box from your Prayer Dice track (Seraph class feature), roll your action die. On 4+, the box is not spent. Additionally, you can use your Sparing Touch feature twice per session instead of once.

**[Mastery]** _Sacred Resonance:_ When you Strike with your Spirit Weapon and score a strong hit with a match, mark progress three times instead of the normal amount.

# Divine Wielder

Play the Divine Wielder if you want to dominate the battlefield with a legendary weapon.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Strength

### FOUNDATION FEATURES

**_Spirit Weapon:_** When you have an equipped weapon with a range of Melee or Very Close, it can fly from your hand to attack an adversary within Close range and then return to you. You can **mark a Stress** to target an additional adversary within range with the same attack roll.

**_Sparing Touch:_** Once per long rest, touch a creature and clear 2 Hit Points or 2 Stress from them.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED FOUNDATION FEATURES

**_Spirit Weapon:_** Your melee weapon can fly from your hand to strike a foe you can see and return to you. When you Strike using your spirit weapon at range, roll +Iron. You may **suffer -1 Spirit** to target an additional foe with the same roll; if you hit, mark progress against both.

**_Sparing Touch:_** Once per session, touch a creature and restore +2 health or +2 Spirit to them (your choice).

### SPECIALIZATION FEATURE

**_Devout:_** When you roll your Prayer Dice, you can roll an additional die and discard the lowest result. Additionally, you can use your "Sparing Touch" feature twice instead of once per long rest.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SPECIALIZATION FEATURE

**_Devout:_** When you spend a box from your Prayer Dice track (Seraph class feature), roll your action die. On 4+, the box is not spent. Additionally, you can use your Sparing Touch feature twice per session instead of once.

### MASTERY FEATURE

**_Sacred Resonance:_** When you roll damage for your "Spirit Weapon" feature, if any of the die results match, double the value of each matching die. For example, if you roll two 5s, they count as two 10s.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED MASTERY FEATURE

**_Sacred Resonance:_** When you Strike with your Spirit Weapon and score a strong hit with a match, mark progress three times instead of the normal amount.

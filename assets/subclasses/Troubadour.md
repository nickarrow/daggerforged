# Troubadour

Play the Troubadour if you want to play music to bolster your allies.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Presence

### FOUNDATION FEATURE

**_Gifted Performer:_** You can play three different types of songs, once each per long rest; describe how you perform for others to gain the listed benefit:

- _Relaxing Song:_ You and all allies within Close range clear a Hit Point.
- _Epic Song:_ Make a target within Close range temporarily _Vulnerable._
- _Heartbreaking Song:_ You and all allies within Close range gain a Hope.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED FOUNDATION FEATURE

**_Gifted Performer:_** You can play three different types of songs, once each per session. Describe how you perform for others to gain the listed benefit:

- _Relaxing Song:_ You and all allies who can hear you take +1 health.
- _Epic Song:_ A target who can hear you is put in a bad spot; the next roll against them gains +1.
- _Heartbreaking Song:_ You and all allies who can hear you gain +1 Hope.

### SPECIALIZATION FEATURE

**_Maestro:_** Your rallying songs steel the courage of those who listen. When you give a Rally Die to an ally, they can gain a Hope or clear a Stress.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SPECIALIZATION FEATURE

**_Maestro:_** Your rallying songs steel the courage of those who listen. When you use your Rally class feature, each ally may also choose to gain +1 Hope or +1 Spirit instead of the momentum.

### MASTERY FEATURE

**_Virtuoso:_** You are among the greatest of your craft and your skill is boundless. You can perform each of your "Gifted Performer" feature's songs twice per long rest.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED MASTERY FEATURE

**_Virtuoso:_** You are among the greatest of your craft and your skill is boundless. You can perform each of your Gifted Performer songs twice per session instead of once.

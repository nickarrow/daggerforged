# Syndicate

Play the Syndicate if you want to have a web of contacts everywhere you go.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Finesse

### FOUNDATION FEATURE

**_Well-Connected:_** When you arrive in a prominent town or environment, you know somebody who calls this place home. Give them a name, note how you think they could be useful, and choose one fact from the following list:

- They owe me a favor, but they'll be hard to find.
- They're going to ask for something in exchange.
- They're always in a great deal of trouble.
- We used to be together. It's a long story.
- We didn't part on great terms.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SUBCLASS ABILITIES

**[Foundation]** _Well-Connected:_ When you arrive in a prominent settlement or location, you know somebody who calls this place home. Give them a name, note how you think they could be useful, and choose one complication: They owe me a favor, but they'll be hard to find / They're going to ask for something in exchange / They're always in a great deal of trouble / We used to be together, it's a long story / We didn't part on great terms. This is a narrative permission — no roll required to establish the contact exists.

**[Specialization]** _Contacts Everywhere:_ Once per session, you can briefly call on a shady contact. Choose one benefit and describe what brought them here: They provide a useful mundane item or a handful of coin (take +2 supply) / Their help gives you an edge (take +2 on your next action roll) / They strike from the shadows (mark progress twice on your current combat objective).

**[Mastery]** _Reliable Backup:_ You can use your Contacts Everywhere feature three times per session. The following options are added to the list: When you suffer harm, they rush out to shield you (reduce the harm by 1) / When you Compel or Gather Information in conversation, they back you up (add +2 and take +1 momentum on a hit).

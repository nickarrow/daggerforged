# School of War

Play the School of War if you want to utilize trained magic for violence.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Knowledge

### FOUNDATION FEATURES

**_Battlemage:_** You've focused your studies on becoming an unconquerable force on the battlefield. Gain an additional Hit Point slot.

**_Face Your Fear:_** When you succeed with Fear on an attack roll, you deal an extra **1d10** magic damage.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SUBCLASS ABILITIES

**[Foundation]** _Battlemage:_ You've focused your studies on becoming an unconquerable force on the battlefield. Gain +1 to your maximum health. _Face Your Fear:_ When you Strike or Clash using magic and your action die shows 1-3, mark progress an additional time on a hit.

**[Specialization]** _Conjure Shield:_ You can maintain a protective barrier of magic. While you have at least 2 Hope, add +1 when you React Under Fire. _Fueled by Fear:_ When you use Face Your Fear (action die shows 1-3 on a magical attack), mark progress twice additional times instead of once on a hit.

**[Mastery]** _Thrive in Chaos:_ When you Strike or Clash and score a hit, you may **suffer -1 Spirit** to mark progress an additional time. _Have No Fear:_ When you use Face Your Fear (action die shows 1-3 on a magical attack), mark progress three times additional times instead of twice on a hit.

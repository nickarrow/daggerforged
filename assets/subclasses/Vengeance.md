# Vengeance

Play the Vengeance if you want to strike down enemies who harm you or your allies.

### FOUNDATION FEATURES

**_At Ease:_** Gain an additional Stress slot.

**_Revenge:_** When an adversary within Melee range succeeds on an attack against you, you can **mark 2 Stress** to force the attacker to mark a Hit Point.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SUBCLASS ABILITIES

**[Foundation]** _At Ease:_ Gain +1 to your maximum Spirit. _Revenge:_ When you suffer harm from a foe at close quarters, you may **suffer -2 Spirit** to immediately mark progress against them.

**[Specialization]** _Act of Reprisal:_ When an adversary harms an ally within close quarters, take +1 on your next Strike or Clash against that adversary.

**[Mastery]** _Nemesis:_ **Spend 2 Hope** to mark an adversary as your Nemesis until the scene ends or you Sojourn. When you Strike or Clash against your Nemesis and score a weak hit, you may treat it as a strong hit. You can only have one Nemesis at a time.

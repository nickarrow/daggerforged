# Wayfinder

Play the Wayfinder if you want to hunt your prey and strike with deadly force.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Agility

### FOUNDATION FEATURES

**_Ruthless Predator:_** When you make a damage roll, you can **mark a Stress** to gain a +1 bonus to your Proficiency. Additionally, when you deal Severe damage to an adversary, they must mark a Stress.

**_Path Forward:_** When you're traveling to a place you've previously visited or you carry an object that has been at the location before, you can identify the shortest, most direct path to your destination.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SUBCLASS ABILITIES

**[Foundation]** _Ruthless Predator:_ When you Strike or Clash and score a hit, you may **suffer -1 Spirit** to mark progress an additional time. Additionally, when you Take Decisive Action and score a strong hit, take +1 momentum. _Path Forward:_ When you're traveling to a place you've previously visited or you carry an object that has been at the location before, you can identify the shortest, most direct path to your destination. This is a narrative permission requiring no roll.

**[Specialization]** _Elusive Predator:_ When you React Under Fire against your Focus (from the Ranger's Focus class feature), add +2.

**[Mastery]** _Apex Predator:_ When you Strike or Clash against your Focus, you may **spend 1 Hope** before rolling. If you score a hit, take +2 momentum.

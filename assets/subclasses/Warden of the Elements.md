# Warden of the Elements

Play the Warden of the Elements if you want to embody the natural elements of the wild.

### SPELLCAST TRAIT

Instinct

### FOUNDATION FEATURE

**_Elemental Incarnation:_** **Mark a Stress** to _Channel_ one of the following elements until you take Severe damage or until your next rest:

- _Fire:_ When an adversary within Melee range deals damage to you, they take **1d10** magic damage.
- _Earth:_ Gain a bonus to your damage thresholds equal to your Proficiency.
- _Water:_ When you deal damage to an adversary within Melee range, all other adversaries within Very Close range must mark a Stress.
- _Air:_ You can hover, gaining advantage on Agility Rolls.

---

#### DAGGERFORGED SUBCLASS ABILITIES

**[Foundation]** _Elemental Incarnation:_ **Suffer -1 Spirit** to _Channel_ one of the following elements until you suffer serious harm (3+) or until the scene ends: _Fire:_ When a foe at close quarters harms you, mark progress against them. _Earth:_ Reduce all harm you suffer by 1 (minimum 1). _Water:_ When you Strike or Clash at close quarters and score a hit, take +1 momentum. _Air:_ You can hover. Add +2 when you roll +Edge.

**[Specialization]** _Elemental Aura:_ Once per scene while Channeling, you can assume an aura matching your element. The aura affects those nearby until your Channeling ends. _Fire:_ When you mark progress against a foe, they are put in a bad spot. _Earth:_ Allies at close quarters add +1 when they roll +Iron. _Water:_ When a foe harms you, you may **suffer -1 Spirit** to reposition them anywhere within close quarters. _Air:_ You and allies at close quarters reduce harm from ranged attacks by 1.

**[Mastery]** _Elemental Dominion:_ You further embody your element. While Channeling, you gain the following benefit: _Fire:_ Add +1 when you Strike or Clash. _Earth:_ When you suffer harm, roll your action die. On 5+, reduce the harm by 1. _Water:_ When you React Under Fire and score a hit, the attacker is put in a bad spot. _Air:_ Add +1 when you React Under Fire. You can fly.
